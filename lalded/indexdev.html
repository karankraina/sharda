Summary
I am pulling my boat (my life) through this ocean (world) by a fragile yarn (my breath / prana). This yarn is so fragile that it can snap out any time. Where and how can my God listen to my prayers and take me across this ocean / world. I feel my life is wasted away just as unbaked earthen plates crumble when in water. My soul feels being in a whirl, getting restless with the immense desire to go my final Adobe to unite with my God.
Additional Commentary
I feel instead of where, it should be how will my god listen to my prayers‚Ä¶..Jyoti Razdan.
If water is filled in unbaked earthen pot, it becomes soft and finally dissolves or breaks and thus the water is wasted. Life here is compared to an unbaked earthen pot and it is said that my life is getting wasted. May be it has to go through the experiance of tapa which would have saved it from getting wasted. It is like fire in a kiln which bakes and makes an earthen pot strong. In the similar way tapa will make one strong and help in reaching the final destination.
