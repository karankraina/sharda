Non-initials (ु) and (ू)


The vowels (ु -> 𑆶) and (ू -> 𑆷) can be used in sharda just as these are used in devnagri i.e. below the consonant. But there are some exceptions which are discussed below.


Example

Hindi Sharda Hindi Sharda
खु 𑆒𑆶 खू 𑆒𑆷
